Vaccination Instructions

Please arrive shortly before the appointment time and bring your identification documents, National ID for Jordanians and passport for others, and bring proof of your affiliation with the chosen work sector (such as a professional ID) and medical reports of chronic diseases (if any).
Please adhere to social distancing and wearing a mask. The vaccination team will guide you with the instructions for the second dose, depending on the type of vaccine.

When you receive the necessary vaccine doses, you will receive an SMS with the link to your vaccination certificate. Please save this link for future reference.

You will receive an SMS with a link to where you can report any side effects which you suspect are associated with taking the vaccine. You can also access this page from your personal profile. COVID-19 vaccines used in this campaign are safe. Regulations mandate a reporting mechanism for suspected side effects.
